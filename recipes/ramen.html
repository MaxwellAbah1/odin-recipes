Ramen Recipe

Description

This is my favorite dish, its a bowl of ramen i cant make it myself so i go to a shop that prepares it, just the way i like.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Ginger
  • Broth
  • Citrus
  • Veggies
  • Egg
  • Chili

Steps in Prearing

  1. Stir-Fry your ingredients
  2. Pour your Broth
  3. Add Noodles
  4. Add Veggies
  5. Top it off with Egg and Chili
